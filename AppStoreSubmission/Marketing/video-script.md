## 30-SECOND SCRIPT

### SCENE 1: Hook (0-5 seconds)
**Visual:** App icon animates in, transforms into brain with glowing neural connections
**Text Overlay:** "CogniXis"
**Audio:** Subtle whoosh sound, electronic pulse
**Voiceover (Optional):** "Train your brain..."

### SCENE 2: Quick Gameplay Montage (5-15 seconds)
**Visual:** Rapid cuts between 4-5 different games
- Color Sequence (tapping colors)
- Speed Math (solving equation)
- Word Scramble (unscrambling)
- Pattern Match (finding pattern)
- Memory Cards (flipping matches)

**Text Overlays (quick succession):**
- "60 Games"
- "60 Seconds"
- "Endless Fun"

**Audio:** Upbeat electronic music, game sound effects

### SCENE 3: Results & Progress (15-22 seconds)
**Visual:** 
- Game completion with stars

**Text Overlay:** "Track Your Progress"
**Audio:** Achievement sound, satisfying completion tone

### SCENE 4: Theme Showcase (22-26 seconds)
**Visual:** Quick morph between all 4 themes
- Cyber Neural â†’ Neon Nights â†’ Gradient Fusion â†’ Dark Matter

**Text Overlay:** "Your Style"
**Audio:** Smooth transition sound

**Visual:**

**Text Overlay:** "Feed Your Brain"
**Audio:** Final musical hit, fade out

## AUDIO GUIDE

### Music
- **Style:** Electronic, upbeat, modern
- **BPM:** 120-130
- **Mood:** Energetic but not aggressive
- **License:** Royalty-free or original

### Sound Effects
| Moment | Sound |
|--------|-------|
| App icon appear | Soft whoosh |
| Game transitions | Quick swoosh |
| Correct answer | Positive ding |
| Score increase | Counting up |
| Mastery unlock | Achievement fanfare |
| Theme change | Smooth morph |
| Final logo | Musical hit |

---

## TEXT OVERLAYS

### Typography
- **Font:** Orbitron Bold (headlines)
- **Size:** Large, readable at small sizes
- **Animation:** Fade in/out, slight scale
- **Duration:** 1.5-2 seconds each

### Key Phrases
1. "60 Games"
2. "60 Seconds"
3. "Track Progress"
4. "Your Style"
5. "Feed Your Brain"

---

## RECORDING TIPS

### Screen Recording
1. Use device's built-in screen recorder
2. Disable notifications
3. Full battery (no charging indicator)
4. Clean, planned interactions
5. Record at native resolution

### Editing
1. Speed up slow moments (1.5x-2x)
2. Cut dead time ruthlessly
3. Add motion blur to transitions
4. Color correct for consistency
5. Export at proper resolution
