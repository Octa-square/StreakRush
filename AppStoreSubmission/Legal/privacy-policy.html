3. How We Store Your Data

3.1 Local Storage

All your game data, preferences, and progress are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage technology. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device
  • We cannot access your data remotely
  • Uninstalling the app removes all data
  • Data does not sync across devices (unless you enable cloud backup)

3.2 No Server Storage

CogniXis does not maintain servers that store your personal gameplay data. The app functions entirely offline.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Remember your preferences (theme, sound, language)
  • Improve the App based on usage patterns
